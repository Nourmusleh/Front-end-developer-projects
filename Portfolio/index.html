GRADUATION PROJECTS

A nursing robot connected to a mobile App that won the best implemented project award of Al-Isra University, 2022. “Dexter” has been also selected to represent Al-Isra University at “ITSAF”, the Information Technology Student Activity Fair that was held in Amman, Jordan, 2022. “Dexter” helps nurses in their daily duties, aiming to maintain their safety level and minimize the direct interaction between the nurse and patient while treating contagious cases, enabling them to collect the patient vitals remotely (blood oxygen level, heartbeat rate, body & room temperature) while handing patients their medicine & food. It also enables nurses to monitor the patient case via Dexter’s web cam that offers a live stream of the patient room. I developed “Dexter” using Arduino Mega, medical sensors (MAX30102 and MLX90614), ESP32 web cam, MQTT protocol, Firebase database, MIT through an application using the MIT App Inventor.

I developed a feasibility study for opening private schools in the villages across the kingdom of Jordan, in which I defined the project vision and mission, scope of work, roles & responsibilities, required job descriptions, collected and analyzed data on the weaknesses and strengths, identified opportunities and risks, drafted the marketing plan and conducted a competitive advantage study.
